Key Features

Industrial workplaces pose risks to both machinery and personnel. Our project aims to mitigate these risks by providing a platform that helps to establish a safer and more secure industrial environment.


Motion Amplification

Experience the power of motion amplification during equipment inspections. Our tool processes videos of working machines, unveiling subtle movements for enhanced maintenance insights.

Emergency Alert

Stay prepared for emergencies with our alert system. In critical situations, individuals can easily signal for help by waving to the nearest camera, triggering an immediate alert.

Restricted Zone

Keep restricted areas secure with live CCTV feeds. Our system detects unauthorized access and raises alerts, ensuring compliance with safety protocols.

Safety gear Detection

Prioritize a safe working environment with our safety gear detection feature. By identifying safety equipment and fire hazards in real-time, we ensure optimal safety conditions for all.
